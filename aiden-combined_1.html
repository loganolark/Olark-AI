How It Works

Seven Steps From First Click to Closed Deal

Aiden Signature and Aiden Bespoke automate the entire intake and handoff workflow — without engineering resources.

Awareness
01

Recognize the Entry Point

Aiden understands and recognizes where a visitor came from — whether it's an ad, an email campaign, or an organic web search.

Personalization
02

Deliver a Tailored Opening

When the conversation opens, the bot provides visitors with specific information tailored to their entry point — not a generic greeting.

Transparency
03

Surface That Context to Your Team

Entry source, intent signals, and conversation history are immediately visible to every rep before they take over.

Enrichment
04

Pull the CRM Record in Real Time

As the visitor enters their information, Aiden simultaneously fetches their contact info, deal stage, and interaction history from your CRM.

Routing
05

Qualify and Route by Territory

Geography, sales identifiers, and team structure determine exactly which rep or department gets the conversation — automatically.

Handoff
06

Arm the Rep with Full Context

When the human moment is ready, the rep receives everything: questions asked, contact origin, past Olark history, and all relevant CRM fields.

Automation
07

Update the CRM Automatically

When the conversation ends, the CRM record is updated with everything that happened. No manual entry. No gaps.
